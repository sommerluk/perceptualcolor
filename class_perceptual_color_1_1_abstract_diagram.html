◆ pageStepChroma

constexpr qreal PerceptualColor::AbstractDiagram::pageStepChroma = 10 * singleStepChroma
staticconstexprprotected

Amount of page step for chroma.

Mesured in LCh chroma units.

The larger of two natural steps that this widget provides and typically corresponds to the user pressing a key or using the mouse wheel: The value will be incremented/decremented by the amount of this value.

The value is 10 times singleStepChroma. This bahaviour corresponds to QAbstractSlider, who’s page step is also 10  bigger than its single step.
